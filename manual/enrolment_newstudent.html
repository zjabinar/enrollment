Enrollment for new student

    Encode student information in 'Student registration' menu and tell student 'StudentID'.
  2. College
    Encode student's class Enrollment in 'Enrollment' menu and give the student 'Enrollment Form'

  3. Cashier
    • Encode student's payment in 'payment' menu.
