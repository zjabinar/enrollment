Beginning of a semester

  1. Administrator
    1. Encode new semeter in 'semester' menu.
    2. Add new course/department in 'course/department' menu (if there are).
  2. Accounting
    1. Encode 'Last day of Enrollment' of the new semester in 'Edit last day of Enrollment' menu.
    2. Encode 'Feerate' of the new semester in 'Edit feerate' menu.
    3. Encode 'Miscellaneous fee' of the new semester in 'Edit miscellaneous fee' menu.
    4. Encode 'Optional fee' of the new semester in 'Edit optional fee' menu.
